About Eduserve

_______________________

On 12th February 2015, Eduserve took shape when three aspiring entrepreneurs came together with a common goal of dedication to philanthropy. Eduserve, a non-profit organization is passionate about educating the under-privileged children. With nobility as a cause, Eduserve contemplates to open the world of knowledge and harness achievers in all walks of life, by empowering children eager to open the doors of success to enhance their lives through education. Eduserve proposes to offer aid, support and assistance, to under-privileged children to enable them to get an education without discrimination on the basis of caste, class, race and religion. By proactively sponsoring them and offering scholarship for orphans and especially those who’s parents are unable to afford to educate them; the purpose will be established. While each child’s completion of education is our main aim, Eduserve strives to support them in terms of aid, until each child is gainfully employed and able to support themselves. In order for these children to outshine, Eduserve , alongside education, endeavors to help enrich emotional well-being and inculcate health care. In the wake of calamities, Eduserve wants to contribute by bearing auxiliary expenses including medical expenses, educational expenses and taking on a social responsibility by creating public awareness and concern towards welfare contributions during such calamity relief in times of famines and flood disasters

What We Want to Accomplish

____________

Refinement being essential, Eduserve plans to actively participate in checking the child`s IQ level and also interest and talents in other activities so that could also be encouraged and developed through enrolling them in various extracurricular activities. Eduserve will spread its wings to meet the children`s teachers and taking them into confidence and enlisting their help and support in the child`s progress. Eduserve proposes to counsel parents by helping them to understand the importance of educating their children in order to give them a better future, and imbue how a good education could change not only the child`s life for the better, but that the entire family would benefit in the long term. Eduserve aims to incorporate opening up a person’s mind to correct factual knowledge to break dogmas and traditions to build up confidence and cohesiveness among children so they can protect themselves from becoming victims of violence and abuse.
